Sprog Gear Library

Sprog is not limited to using the 'gears' (reusable components) that come bundled in the distribution. You can write your own gears and share them with the world.

There are plans afoot to create a repository of Sprog gears. In the mean time, this page will list links to 'third party' gears. To get listed here, just email the URL from which your gear can be downloaded, to: grantm@cpan.org.
